Yes, within reason. Initial call is free, and we'll answer general questions via email. For detailed architecture discussions, we typically need to be under NDA.

### "Can we meet in person?"
Yes, if you're Miami-based or we're traveling to your city. Initial calls are typically video, but happy to meet in person for final delivery or kickoff.

We accommodate all time zones for initial calls and engagements. Most clients are US-based, but we've worked with EMEA and APAC companies.

Not typically, unless you're an active engagement with an emergency. Weekday response times are reliable.
